Deltares offers various solutions to manage and host your project data. The most suitable data solution depends on many factors, e.g. data type, volume of data, internal/external access, performance, retention, etc.  

The Deltares Data Storage Finder is a tool that helps you in getting an overview of the possible data/IT solutions that can be used for data storage and data management within Deltares. By answering the questions about your demands, suggested data solutions that meet your needs will be highlighted. Clicking on a solution will provide you with more details about the solution.
